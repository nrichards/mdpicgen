## BASIC SHORTCUTS

Button | Overview | Function
------------ | -- | -------------
SHIFT + MODE PLAY | ![](./manual_images/but/s_mplay.png) | Dump current preset to MIDI OUT. This is useful to transfer the current preset to another Qun / Qun mk2 synthesizer.
SHIFT + SYSTEM | ![](./manual_images/but/s_sys.png) | All notes off
SHIFT + PARAM | ![](./manual_images/but/s_param.png) | Initialize the preset. Choose from a default: Blank, Sampler, EvenSlice, or Scratch. Please see the [Initialize preset](#initialize-preset) section for details. 
SHIFT + SEQ PLAY + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/s_splay_d.png) | Set BPM. Tap tempo by pressing SEQ PLAY 4 or more times while you keep pressing SHIFT. 
MODE PLAY + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/mplay_d.png) | Record volume -- stay at -6.0dB or lower to avoid clipping. _Tutorial: [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7dNhxKBtwPo&t=770s) is available._
MODE PLAY + SYSTEM + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/mplay_sys_d.png) | Looper master volume 
SYSTEM + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/sys_d.png) | VCF volume
PARAM + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/param_d.png) | VCF cutoff
REC + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/lr_d.png) | LFO tune
LOOPER PLAY + [1-5] | ![](./manual_images/but/lplay_1..5.png) | Select Scene
REC + [1-3, 7,8] | ![](./manual_images/but/lr_1..378.png) | Select looper sample recording track. A,B,C are mono tracks. Press 7, 8 for stereo recording. 7=A(Left)+B(Right), 8 = B+C. When you do stereo recording, you may want to set pan to left and right for the selected stereo tracks. 
PARAM + [1-3] | ![](./manual_images/but/param_1..3.png) | Mute track
MODE PLAY (RECALL) + [1-8] | ![](./manual_images/but/mplay_1..8.png) | Recall preset bucket. See the [Preset Bucket](#preset-bucket) section for details.
SEQ PLAY + [1-8] | ![](./manual_images/but/mplay_1..8_d.png) | Select Sequencer pattern
SHIFT + LOOPER PLAY + turn dial | ![](./manual_images/but/s_lplay_d.png) | Load session when you are not in System mode or Granular mode. 
SHIFT + LOOPER PLAY + [1-3] button | ![](./manual_images/but/s_lplay_1..3.png) | Import WAV file from import folder to selected track
SYSTEM + [1-8] | ![](./manual_images/but/sys_1..8.png) | Temporary piano mode
